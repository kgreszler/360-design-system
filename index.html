Happening

Happening is an event-production company that puts together concerts, parties, shows, and more. They are focused on promoting both their events and their services as second-to-none for a wide variety of brands and clients. Their image is energetic, youthful, and fun.

About Us

Happening started out as a way for people to come together and enjoy the vibrant and uniqueness of Austin, Texas. The Pennybaker Bridge aka the 360 Bridge in Austin was built to connect the northern and southern sections of the loop 360 highway. The 360 Design System, like the bridge connects and encompasses the design needs of Happening and their clients
